Welcome to the Invasive Insects Database

This website serves as a comprehensive resource for information about invasive insect species in the United States. Here, you can explore various species, their habitats, and the counties where they have been spotted. Our goal is to raise awareness about invasive insects and provide valuable information to help manage their impact on local ecosystems and agriculture.

Use the navigation bar above to access our database of invasive insects, view an interactive map to see where different species are located, or report any sightings you may encounter. Your contributions will help us keep our community informed and protect our natural resources.
